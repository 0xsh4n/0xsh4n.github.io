About Me

Download Resume

Hello, I'm Shan, a passionate Computer Science Engineering student from South India. My journey into the world of technology began at a young age, and since then, I've been captivated by the endless possibilities it offers.

I specialize in Cyber Security and have a keen interest in discovering vulnerabilities and crafting innovative solutions to address them. My dedication to staying updated with the latest trends and technologies in the field allows me to tackle complex challenges head-on.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I am actively involved in various projects and initiatives aimed at enhancing cybersecurity awareness and promoting ethical hacking practices. I believe in the power of collaboration and am always eager to connect with like-minded individuals who share my passion for technology.

In my free time, you'll often find me participating in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, engaging in bug bounty programs, or exploring new tools and techniques to sharpen my skills. I'm also an avid reader and enjoy delving into cybersecurity-related literature to expand my knowledge further.
